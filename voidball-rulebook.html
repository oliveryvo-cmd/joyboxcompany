WHAT IS VOIDBALL?

VOIDBALL is a fast-paced outdoor team sport played on a circular field with three zones. Two teams of 5 compete to score points by throwing the Orb into the center zone — called The Void. The first team to reach 10 points wins.

The key twist: you cannot run while holding the Orb. Every move must be a pass. Strategy, teamwork, and speed are everything.

THE FIELD
Official VOIDBALL Field Diagram

The field is a large circle divided into 3 zones. The Outer Ring is where most play happens. The Middle Ring is the attack zone. The Void (center circle) is the scoring zone, guarded by the Void Guardian.

OFFICIAL RULES
01
The Orb
One ball — called the Orb — is used per match. You cannot run while holding the Orb. You must pass it to advance it across the field. You may pivot on one foot while holding it.
02
Scoring
Throw the Orb into The Void (center circle) = 1 point. If a teammate is STANDING in The Void when the Orb lands there = 3 points (Void Strike).
03
The Void Guardian
Each team has one Void Guardian who defends The Void. The Guardian cannot leave the center zone. Every 2 minutes, teams must rotate a new player into the Guardian role.
04
Defense
Defenders may intercept passes and block throws. No physical contact with the Orb carrier. The Orb may only be stolen from the air — never grabbed from another player's hands.
05
Winning
First team to reach 10 points wins the match. If tied at 10, play sudden death — the next team to score wins.
06
Reset After Score
After each score, both teams reset to their side of the Outer Ring. The team that was scored on starts with the Orb for the next play.
POSITIONS
POSITION ROLE ZONE
Void Guardian Defends The Void. Rotates every 2 minutes. Most critical role. Center Zone only
Striker Main scorer. Positions inside The Void to receive 3-point passes. Middle Ring / Void
Runner Moves the Orb fast with quick passes. Never holds longer than 2 seconds. Outer + Middle Ring
Blocker Guards the outer ring. Intercepts enemy passes. Protects the Runner. Outer Ring
Anchor All-around player. Switches between attack and defense freely. Full field
SCORING SUMMARY
ACTION POINTS NOTES
Orb thrown into The Void 1 Standard score
VOID STRIKE — teammate in The Void catches Orb 3 Highest scoring play
First to 10 points WIN Sudden death if tied at 10
EQUIPMENT
  • One ball (the Orb) — any size ball works to start
  • Cones or chalk to mark the 3 field zones
  • 10 players (2 teams of 5)
  • Open outdoor space (grass, concrete, park)
  • Optional: timer for Guardian rotations
